# Microsoft Purview Data Lifecycle Management and Retention



## Links

- [Microsoft Learn](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/purview/data-lifecycle-management)


## Notes

Purview > Solutions > Data Lifecycle Management

- Enforces Retention Policies for 
  - Teams
  - OneDrive
  - Exchange
  - Etc

- Retention Policies
  - Adaptive or Static locations
    - You choose static locations
  - Determine if you want content retained for a specific period
  - Determine if you want content deleted after a specific period
- Retention Labels
  - Used for exceptions to retention policy
- Records management
  - Enhanced Retention Labels
- ** If you have multiple policies for labels for an item retention takes precidence**
- ** If you have multiple retention labels or policies for an item the latest date takes precidence**
- ** If you have multiple deletion labels or policies labels take precidence over policies**
- Automated policies
- Proof of compliance
