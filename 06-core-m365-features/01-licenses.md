# Explain how license types assigned to users and groups affect access to Microsoft 365 features

## Links

- [Microsoft Learn](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/commerce/licenses/subscriptions-and-licenses?view=o365-worldwide)

## Notes

- License Types
  - Basic plans usually do not include desktop versions
  - Business
    - Have **Business** in title
    - Office 365 Business
    - Up to 300 Users
  - Individual
  - Enterprise
    - Have **E** in title
    - Office 365 E5
  - Academic
    - Have **A** in title
    - Office 365 A3
- Subscriptions
  - Subscriptions are needed to access M365 features
  - Types of subscriptions determine what features you can access
