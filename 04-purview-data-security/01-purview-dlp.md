# Microsoft Purview Data Loss Prevention (DLP)


## Links

- [Microsoft Learn](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/purview/dlp-learn-about-dlp)


## Notes

- Data Loss Prevention
  - Purview > Solutions > Data Loss Prevention

- Prevents users from sharing sensitive information 
- Protects data in transit, at rest, and in use
- Integrates with classification and labeling

- Policies
  - Identify what is sensitive data
    - Determine what type of information you want to protect
      - Apps and Devices
      - Inline web traffic
        - Edge business browser
  - How to protect it
  - Admin units 
    - Who you want the policy assigned to
      - Users or Groups
  - Where to apply policy too
  - Define policy settings
  - What info to protect
  - Policy can be ran in simulation mode or saved as a draft
  - 2-24 hours for policy to take effect
  - Simulation Mode
    - You can view results of simulation
