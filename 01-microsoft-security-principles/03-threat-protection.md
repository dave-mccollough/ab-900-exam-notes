## Notes

- XDR = Extendend Detection and Response

- Device protection
  - Defender for Endpoints
- Identities
  - Entra ID
- SaaS apps
  - Defender for Cloud Apps
- Email and Collaboration Tools
  - Defender for Office 365

- Threat hunting
- Automated responses to threats
- Free with E5 or A5 licenses
- Can be purchased as an add-on
